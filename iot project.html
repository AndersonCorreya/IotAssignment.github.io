About Us

The Raspberry Pi Foundation is a non-profit organization that empowers individuals worldwide to learn, create, and innovate through its low-cost, credit-card-sized computers, fostering digital literacy and technology education

Raspberry Pi functions as a compact, low-cost computer capable of various tasks, including coding, multimedia playback, and IoT projects, offering versatility and accessibility for tech enthusiasts and educators
